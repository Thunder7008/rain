The Beginning

June

Finally, abh decide kar liya tha apni feelings ke barein mein batane ke liye, iss time tak confirm hogya tha ki yeh feeling mutual hai. Sabse pehle June ki staring ke liye socha tha, par tabh 4 th ko test tha allen mein aur tavishi di ka advance bhi tha, fir decide kiya ki Monday 5 th June ko sahi rahega. Fir 5 th aagyi, aur meri fay gyi, aapko bhi tabh tak pta chal gay tha shayad ki kya chal rha hai, itna obvious kar diya tha mene, aur arnav abhi aapse iss barein mein puchte ja rha tha. Usse mene hee kaha tha puchne ke liya, classes ke baad jabh ham rukte the, 2-3 hafto se yahi sabh discuss kar rhe tha ham. First class ke baad aap piche aaye the mere saath baithne ke liye, aur dono ek dusre ko dekh kar sharme rhe the, dono ki same feelings thi, bas kuch keh nahi rhe the ham, bohot pyara time tha voh. Mein tabh pura blank hogya tha, samaj hee nahi aarha tha ki kya karu, isliye mein peeche arihant aur arnav se notebook pe likh likh ke baat kar rha tha, inti stupid harkat thi voh, aapko bhi pta tha, pta nahi tabh aapko kya laga hoga, par joh bhi, its one of my fondest memories. Last ke dono classes bhi khatam hogyi thi, aur mein kuch nahi keh paya, baad mein arnav ko kaha ki shubhika se kahe aapko bulane ke liye, aur us time joh meri gandi fat rhi thi na… Aur meri itni aachi qismat, window ke paas jaise hee aap neeche aaye, vahan se fauji aagya, sabh kabada hoagya, meri hamare aur fauji ka rishta itna aacha tha vaise bhi, class mein jaake bohot sunaya usne. Abh dikkat ke yeh thi ki itni bade blunder ke baad vapas kaise approach karta, aise proposal shayad hee kisi ka hoga. Abh aayi 6 th June, is baad aar ya paar, claases ke time toh kuch baat nahi hui hamari, bas dono beech beech mein ek dusre ko dekhe ja rhe the. Classes khatam hone ke baad, it was the time and neeche jaate time, finally keh diya, “I really like you” aur tbh mujhe pta tha aap nah toh nahi kahoge, haan toh hai, par joh aapne reply kiya tha voh mein kabhi nahi socha tha. “Mujhe pta hai”, iska kya hee reply deta, aur kuch kaha bhi nahi aapne itni mein ham neeche pounch gye aur fir aapne kaha ki apne rooms mein chale jaate hai, koi dekh lega, kal vale blunder ke baad. I was stun, mujhe nahi samaj aay ki kya karu kya kahu, ek moment pe toh laga ki sabh khatam hogya, tabhi bali ne aake btya ki aap bula rhe ho, fir aachanak se mujhe rainbow dikhne laga aur mein hawa mein udne laga… Agle moment mein aap mere samne the aur tabh mujhe pta chala ki aapse bhi nahi bola ja rha tha pehle, ham dono hee fatu hai. Then I heard those magical words “I also like you”. Mera dil pighal gya, bohot bohot zyada khush tha mein. Fir ham bad 101 mein jaake sath baith gye, and aur beautiful journey started on Tuesday, 6 th June 2023 .

July

  • 21 st July vali baat toh shayad yaad hogi aapko, jabh mein aap aur kanha DC mein baithe the, mein aapke opposite tha, tabh aap table ke neeche se mujhe book le rhe the, tabh aapka hath mujhe touch hogya tha, uff… joh fati thi meri tabh. Ofc kisi galat way mein nahi, bas aachanak se aapke sparsh se mera maan vichlit hogya tha. Yeh toh pakka nahi bhule hoge aap, aur mein bhi nahi bhul sakta kabhi.

  • Iss time tak hamari bohot saari batein ho rhi thi, we were getting closer and closer. Ek din ham allen mein kaafi zyada der tak ruke hue the, us din shaam ko ham aise hee batein kar rhe the, tabhi aapka message aaya, “I love being with you, I love taking to you”. Can’t tell you kitni khushi mili thi, us time yeh bilkul expect nahi kiya tha mene. Jabh aapse pucha, tabh aapne yahi kaha ki aaj kaafi der sath time bitaya, isliye.

  • Iss josh josh mein mene ek din “Goodnight sweetheart” likha tha, btw which I mean from bottom of my heart, us din toh neend mein aapne dhyan nahi diya par agle din aap iske barein mein kuch rhe the, ig aapko itna pasand nahi aaya tha yeh. Par iske kuch dino baad hee aapne yeh message kiya tha, so pta nahi aapko yeh naam pasand aaya tha ya nahi, but I like calling you sweetheart.

  • Yeh month jaise bhi gya ho, but the end… was magical. 27 th July ko bhi ham allen mein der tak ruke hue the, us din aap mujhe magnetism ke advance ke pyq karne ko de rhe the, aur har sahi answer pe ek truth ya dare the. Us din ki vibe totally alag thi, I could tell aap chahte the ki mein sahi answer nikalu, kaafi hints de rhe the aap, samaj rha tha mein ki yeh kis direction mein ja rha hai, but I was not sure. Maan mein bas yahi dar tha ki aap comfortable rho bas. Tabh mujhe 3 options mile the, (btw usme se aapke liye ek dare mera abhi bhi bacha hua hai). Ek time ke liye mene socha bhi tha, fir vohi baat sochke nahi bola. Vaise iss din hamne pehli baar sath mein picture li thi.
