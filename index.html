The Beginning

June

Finally, abh decide kar liya tha apni feelings ke barein mein batane ke liye, iss time tak confirm hogya tha ki yeh feeling mutual hai. Sabse pehle June ki staring ke liye socha tha, par tabh 4th ko test tha allen mein aur tavishi di ka advance bhi tha, fir decide kiya ki Monday 5th June ko sahi rahega. Fir 5th aagyi, aur meri fay gyi, aapko bhi tabh tak pta chal gay tha tabh tak shayad ki kya chal rha hai, itna obvious kar diya tha mene, aur arnav abhi aapse iss barein mein puchte ja rha tha. Usse mene hee kaha tha puchne ke liya, classes ke baad jabh ham rukte the, 2-3 hafto se yahi sabh discuss kar rhe tha ham. First class ke baad aap piche aaye the mere saath baithne ke liye, aur dono ek dusre ko dekh kar sharme rhe the, ham dono ki same feelings thi, bas kuch keh nahi rhe the ham, bohot pyara time tha voh. Mein tabh pura blank hogya tha, samaj hee nahi aarha tha ki kya karu, isliye mein peeche arihant aur arnav se notebook pe likh likh ke baat kar rha tha, inti stupid harkat thi voh, aapko bhi pta tha, pta nahi tabh aapko kya laga hoga, par joh bhi, its one of my fondest memories. Last ke dono classes bhi khatam hogyi thi, aur mein kuch nahi keh paya, baad mein arnav ko kaha ki shubhika se kahe aapko bulane ke liye, aur us time joh meri gandi fat rhi thi na… Aur meri itni aachi qismat, window ke paas jaise hee aap neeche aaye, vahan se fauji aagya, sabh kabada hoagya, meri hamare aur fauji ka rishta itna aacha tha vaise bhi, class mein jaake bohot sunaya usne. Abh dikkat ke yeh thi ki itni bade blunder ke baad vapas kaise approach karta, aise proposal shayad hee kisi ka hoga. Abh aayi 6th June, is baad aar ya paar, claases ke time toh kuch baat nahi hui hamari, bas dono beech beech mein ek dusre ko dekhe ja rhe the. Classes khatam hone ke baad, it was the time and neeche jaate time, finally keh diya, “I REALLY LIKE YOU”
